Frequently Asked Questions

How often are we billed?

For the annual plan, you are billed upfront for the year, but if you choose to use the pay-as-you-go plan, you will be billed weekly according to your order volume.

I don't know technology, how does this all work?

Well, if you found this site, you're savvy enough to use our software. We setup all the crazy server stuff for you and let the magic happen. You just need to take care of a few settings on the site and restaurant info, and users ordering and everything else will be ready to go.

Why should I use your software over another company's?

That's an easy one. We have not only had first-hand experience in the restaurant delivery industry, but our software is built using the most modern principles in both design and programming. Our mission is to empower businesses with the most powerful tools to run their delivery business, and we're constantly working on developing new solutions and improving on existing ones. With cloud servers from Amazon and a team of software engineers, our software has guarenteed performance and satisfaction on all fronts.

Why are you called Nucleus?

Contrary to popular belief, this name wasn't directly inspired by the company in HBO's Silicon Valley. We call ourselves Nucleus because we plan to power the very core of your business. Plus, it sounds cool and scientific.

Do you provide software solutions for any other contexts other than restaurant delivery?

Unfortunately, as of right now, we are focusing on restaurant delivery and plan on mastering that sector before moving into anything else. Logistics and courier services are becoming more popular and not too different from food delivery, so we will be offering more specialized software solutions in the near future. But, if you do have a unique use case, feel free to contact us anyway and we'll see if we can make something work.

What kind of businesses can use this software?

We designed this software for companies that are delivering for several different restaurants - basically 3rd party restaurant delivery companies. The software is flexible though, so if you have another use case that you are considering, feel free to contact us anyway.

Oh, how does the driver dispatching and restaurant notification work?

That's a good question. The system is all automated so it's set to send SMS messages to restaurants and drivers whenever an order is placed. You can elect to call drivers and restaurants if you want, but we find that this system is more effective for busy hours. We plan on implementing a more advanced system of mobile apps and tablets soon, so stay posted!
